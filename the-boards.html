The Core
Keep it simple · Clear action, clear want. Lean into familiar characters, scenarios, and archetypes.
Keep it tight · Feel permission to end your scene, and move on to the next. No reason for a scene to drag.
Keep it heightening · Let the love and hate build and grow. Don't interrupt wooing scenes or fights.
Keep it crisp · Listen hard and have clean give and take.
